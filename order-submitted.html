Prep & pack like a pro

Follow this checklist to avoid delays and keep your payout on track.

Prepare your device

  • Back up your data and sign out of Apple ID/iCloud or Google/Samsung accounts.
  • Turn off Find My iPhone / Activation Lock or Factory Reset Protection (FRP).
  • Remove SIM and eSIM profiles, cases, and accessories you want to keep.
  • Perform a full factory reset so the device is ready for inspection.

Pack safely

  • Wrap the device in bubble wrap or a soft cloth to prevent movement.
  • Use a sturdy box or the provided kit mailer; fill empty space with padding.
  • Include the device ID sticker from your kit or a note with your order number.
